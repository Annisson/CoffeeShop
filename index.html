Who are we?

Founded by best friends Zahra Khalifa and Alicia Wilde, we specialize in providing premium coffee offerings and cutting-edge machines to elevate your business. Our professional environment values clients as partners, ensuring the enjoyment of life's simple pleasures one cup at a time.

We specialize in delivering high-quality coffee and cutting-edge coffee machines to businesses, ensuring they're equipped with the finest brews. Additionally, our coffee parties cater to both private gatherings and corporate events, offering an unforgettable experience of aromatic delights and social connection.
