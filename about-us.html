About us

About us - The story of Brew & Cocoa Co.

Our story is one of friendship, passion, and a shared love for the simple joys in life. Founded by best friends Alicia Wilde and Zahra Khalifa, our journey began in college where our bond over coffee and chocolate blossomed. Through the ups and downs of life, we have remained best friends and each others biggest supporters, cherishing calm and happy moments amidst the chaos that is life. Inspired by our enduring friendship and mutual dedication to delivering excellence in every cup, we founded Brew & Cocoa Co. as a premier destination for businesses seeking top-quality coffee solutions. Here, businesses are assured of unparalleled service and products tailored to their needs, fostering a partnership built on trust and reliability.

Join us as we brew memories, one sip at a time, in a world that can always use a little more joy and connection.

Zahra Nouri - CEO & Co-founder

Drawing from her entrepreneurial experience, Zahra envisioned Brew & Cocoa Co. as more than just a coffee supplier; a provider of taste experiences tailored to elevate business environments, inspired by the ability of simple pleasures. Zahra's dream is to cultivate a space where businesses thrive and lasting impressions are brewed with every cup.
