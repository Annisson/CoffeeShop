About us

About us - The story of Bean Bliss Café

Our story is one of friendship, passion, and a shared love for the simple joys in life. Founded by best friends Alicia Wilde and Zahra Khalifa, our journey began in college where our bond over coffee and chocolate blossomed. Through the ups and downs of life, we have remained best friends and each others biggest supporters, cherishing calm and happy moments amidst the chaos that is life. Inspired by our enduring friendship and mutual devotion to crafting the perfect cup, we founded Bean Bliss Café as a haven where everyone is welcomed into our extended family.

Join us as we brew memories, one sip at a time, in a world that can always use a little more joy and connection.

Zahra Nouri - CEO & Co-founder

Drawing from her entrepreneurial experience with a history of successful start-ups, Zahra envisioned Bean Bliss Café as more than just a coffee shop; it's a haven where every sip narrates tales of joy and connection. Inspired by the ability of these simple pleasures to elevate spirits, Zahra's dream is to cultivate a space where community thrives and lasting memories are brewed one cup at a time.
